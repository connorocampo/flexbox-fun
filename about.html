Our Story

Charm City Burger Company may not look like much from the outside, but it's what we do on the inside that sets us apart from the rest. A 100% Chef-driven concept, our burgers use the finest ingredients with a wide array of options so you're sure to find something to suit your taste. Our proprietary 1/3lb Charm City Burger Blend is made right here in South Florida, ground daily in the Burger Lab at Sunshine Provisions, a USDA facility in Hallandale Beach. We are proud to offer you 5 star chef designed burgers at a quick service price.
